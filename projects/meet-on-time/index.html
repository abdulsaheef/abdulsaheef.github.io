How to use Meet on Time

Add Cities

Search for cities worldwide to compare their timezones. Start typing a city name and select from the suggestions.

Use the presets or customize working hours to see when your team is available.

Drag to Select

Click and drag across the timeline to see what time it would be in each city.

Find Overlaps

The system automatically highlights times when all team members are available.
